2. Why are many features unavailable?
- Some features are still in testing and are temporarily disabled.
- Other features are currently in development and will be released in upcoming updates.

3. Why can RAYA answers feel unusual sometimes?
- RAYA is built as a study assistant and coach, not only an answer generator.
- It guides users through reasoning to improve understanding, memory, and long-term learning.
- Depending on context, tone can shift between supportive, motivating, direct, or challenging while staying respectful.

4. Why do I have usage limits?
- During launch/testing, resources are limited, so daily limits help maintain fair access and platform stability.
- The team is actively working to increase limits over time.

5. Upgrade plans
- Paid plans are designed to unlock higher usage limits and advanced features.
- Plans are not available yet, but they are planned for future releases.
- For minors, parent/guardian supervision is strongly recommended for any payment flow.

6. For institutions
- RAYA supports schools with class-level progress insights and learning gap analysis.
- Student data is anonymized and aggregated to protect privacy.
- Only relevant, non-personal information is shared with institutions.
